Usability: GE Appliances
Cafe SCAA Coffee Maker

Design Background & Objective


GE Appliances made a bold and strategic move from a largely single GE brand focus to a unique house of brands umbrella, which gave Monogram®, GE Café™, GE®, GE Profile™, Haier and Hotpoint their own identities among the owner segments they were crafted to serve.


GE Cafe's target audience are those who are feature seekers. It aims at providing customizable appliances with luxury style. SCAA coffee maker is one of the products in the small appliances product line under GE Cafe, designed to bring minimal but luxury coffee making experience. SCAA refers to Specialty Cofee Association of America. SCAA coffee science experts and professional coffee tasters developed a series of rigorous requirements to ensure the best brewed coffee quality.


I joined the design of the coffee maker in July 2019 where most of the industrial design had been determined. However, there's a large gap in the user experience and usability of UI before the launch of the product. My goals were to:

- Identify usability issues in the user flow

- Deliver high fidelity workable prototype or usability testing

- Pinpoint user experience issues from prototyping and usability testing

- Reiterate the design of the coffee maker UI
